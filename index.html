Game Rules

1. Dice Mechanics & Values

  • Dice Type: Two 4-sided binary dice (Faces: 1, 2, 3, 0).
  • Scoring: Sum of two dice. 0 + 0 = 12.
  • Extra Turns: Roll 1, 5, 6, or 12 to roll again.
  • Turn Limit: Max 5 rolls per turn to prevent loops.

2. Movement & Entry

  • Entry Rule (Thayam): You must roll a 1 to enter the board.
  • Buffer System: Roll first, build a buffer, then distribute moves freely.
  • Safe Zones: Squares with a Star () are safe from capture.

3. Capturing (Vettu) & Progression

  • The Cut (Vettu): Land on an opponent in a non-safe zone to send them home.
  • The "Lock" Rule: You MUST cut an opponent to unlock the inner "Home Run" path.

4. Winning

  • The Goal: Reach the center square, the Pazham (Fruit).
  • Exact Roll: You need an exact roll to land on the center to win.
